May 2026
App Distribution

Multi-Region App Store Distribution Program Fully Operational

Jiexiaokui has completed its structured expansion of mobile management app deployment to Google Play and Apple App Store across five major regions: the United States, United Kingdom, European Union, Canada, and Australia.

This expansion required a comprehensive audit of each application's data safety form, app privacy label, age rating, advertising disclosure, and consent management implementation. All apps now meet the data collection and use requirements mandated by regional data protection authorities in each market.

The program includes ATT framework compliance for iOS deployments, Google Play Data Safety section accuracy reviews, and verified alignment with each platform's developer policies for ad content, ad frequency, and user data handling.

Compliance and Operations

Policy and Standards Updates

We monitor major app store policies, advertising platform rules, and data protection regulations continuously to keep every product and service in alignment.

App Store Policy Alignment

Our mobile management app review process tracks updates to:

  • Google Play Developer Policy updates (ads, data, age, permissions)
  • Apple App Store Review Guidelines (Section 5, privacy, ads)
  • Data Safety Form accuracy requirements for Google Play
  • App Privacy Nutrition Label accuracy for App Store
  • Google Play Families Policy for apps accessible to children
  • ATT and consent SDK integration for iOS

Regional Data Protection Law Monitoring

Active tracking and readiness for regulatory changes under:

  • EU GDPR and national DPA guidance (France, Germany, Netherlands)
  • UK GDPR and ICO guidance updates
  • US state privacy laws: CCPA, CPRA, VCDPA, CPA, CTDPA
  • Canada PIPEDA and provincial privacy law developments
  • Brazil LGPD enforcement and regulatory guidance
  • Australian Privacy Act and draft reform proposals
